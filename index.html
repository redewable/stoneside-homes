01 Our Heritage

Four Decades of Unwavering
Craftsmanship

1984

When most builders were chasing volume, we chose a different path—one measured not in units completed, but in families served with excellence.

For forty years, Stoneside has built custom homes in Bryan–College Station with the same philosophy: direct communication, honest numbers, and craftsmanship that stands the test of time. We've never advertised. Our homes—and the families who live in them—have always been our best ambassadors.

Every Stoneside home carries our family name. That means something to us. It means we build as if we'll be the ones living there. It means we stand behind our work long after the keys are handed over.

Patience Over Speed

We take on fewer projects by design. This allows us to give each home the attention it deserves—and each family the communication they need.

Transparent Process

Clear scope. Honest pricing. No hidden costs or surprise change orders. We believe trust is built before the foundation is poured.

Enduring Quality

We choose materials and methods that age gracefully. No trends. No shortcuts. Just homes built to serve families for generations.
